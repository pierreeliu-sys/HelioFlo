Key Features

40,000 Wp solar input across 3 MPPTs

Three independent MPPT inputs handle up to 40,000 Wp of solar panels — ideal for large roof areas or properties with multiple panel orientations maximising generation at all times of day.

≤ 4 ms blackout switchover

Your home transfers to battery power in under 4 milliseconds during a grid outage — before most sensitive appliances register any interruption.

Parallel master-slave stacking

Multiple units can be combined in a master-slave configuration, scaling both AC output and storage capacity to match the demands of larger properties without a system redesign.

Integrated AC charger for EVs

Built-in AC charging capability supports EV charging directly from stored solar energy, eliminating the need for a separate EV charger unit.

43,000 W on-grid AC output

Capable of exporting up to 43 kW back to the grid or powering high-demand applications, with a maximum phase output current of up to 30.3 A.

Available in 10, 15, and 20 kW configurations. Federal rebate may apply under the Cheaper Home Batteries Program.
