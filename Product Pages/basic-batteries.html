Key Features

Plug and play installation

Pre-made battery, meter, and CAN cabling eliminates complex wiring. Automatic battery identification means the inverter configures itself to the PowerBox Pro without manual setup.

LiFePO4 — 6,000+ cycle life

Lithium iron phosphate chemistry is the safest available for residential installation: no thermal runaway risk, a 10-year warranty, and ≥ 6,000 charge cycles at 90% depth of discharge.

Blackout backup (< 10 ms)

Automatic UPS switching keeps essential circuits powered during grid outages. The S6 LV transfers to battery power in under 10 ms — fast enough for most sensitive appliances.

Dual MPPT for multiple orientations

Two independent MPPTs let you connect panels on different roof faces or orientations without loss from mismatched strings — maximising solar yield throughout the day.

Expandable to 50 units

The PowerBox Pro supports up to 50 units in parallel. Start with one 10.2 kWh battery and add capacity as your energy needs or budget grow — without replacing the inverter.

Government rebate eligible

All HelioFlo-installed batteries are eligible for the federal Cheaper Home Batteries Program rebate, reducing your upfront cost significantly.

Is essential right for you?

The right entry point into solar storage.

The PowerBox Pro is a proven low-voltage LiFePO4 system that works across a wide range of homes and budgets. Its safe, compact design and flexible expansion mean you can start with what you need today and scale as your energy use or ambitions grow.

Smaller households

1–2 person households or apartments typically consume less than 15 kWh per day, making a 10.2 kWh battery at 90% DoD a meaningful and cost-effective addition.

Lower upfront investment

The low-voltage PowerBox Pro has a lower entry price than high-voltage systems, making solar storage accessible even when budget is a primary consideration.

Starter energy independence

Stop exporting solar energy for next to nothing. Store it in the PowerBox Pro and use it in the evening, reducing your reliance on grid power at peak rates.

Future expandability

Up to 50 PowerBox Pro units can be connected in parallel. Add capacity as your needs or budget grow without replacing the inverter or starting the system from scratch.
