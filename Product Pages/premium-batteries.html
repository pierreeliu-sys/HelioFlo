Key Features

Modular from 5.1 kWh

Start with a 5.1 kWh battery and scale all the way to 45.9 kWh without replacing existing components. A flexible investment that grows with your energy needs.

3-phase whole-home coverage

True 3-phase AC output means every circuit in your home is covered, including heavy loads like air conditioning, ovens, and pool pumps.

Integrated fire prevention

Active aerosol fire prevention module is built directly into the battery system, providing an additional layer of protection beyond the inherent safety of LFP chemistry.

Smart monitoring

Real-time monitoring and control via the EcoFlow app. Track solar generation, battery state of charge, and grid usage from anywhere on your phone.

Blackout backup

Automatically switches to battery power during grid outages, keeping your home fully powered with no perceptible interruption to your appliances.

A premium high-capacity system is the right choice when maximum self-sufficiency, EV charging, or extended backup duration is the priority.

Charge your electric vehicle entirely from solar energy stored during the day. A premium system stores enough to fuel both your home and your car.
