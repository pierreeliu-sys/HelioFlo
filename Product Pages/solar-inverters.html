Key Features

Instant blackout backup

Switches from grid to battery power in under 10 milliseconds, fast enough that most appliances don't notice the transition.

Hybrid operation

Operates on-grid, off-grid, or in hybrid mode. Automatically optimises between solar generation, battery storage, and grid import to minimise your electricity bill.

Dual MPPT inputs

Two independent maximum power point trackers allow panels on different roof orientations or with different shading profiles to operate at peak efficiency simultaneously.

Wi-Fi monitoring

Real-time energy monitoring via the SolisCloud app. Track solar generation, battery state, and grid usage from anywhere on your phone.

Australian grid compliant

CEC-approved and fully compliant with AS4777-2:2020 and IEEE 2030-5, ensuring safe and legal grid connection across all Australian states and territories.

Why it matters

Your inverter determines everything.

The inverter is the most critical component in a solar battery system. A high-quality hybrid inverter maximises your solar self-consumption, protects your home during grid outages, and forms the foundation for expanding your energy storage over time.

Maximum solar harvest

Dual MPPT technology extracts the most power from your solar panels even under partial shading or mixed roof orientations.

Blackout protection

A hybrid inverter with under 4 ms switchover means your home stays powered during grid outages without any perceptible interruption.
