Australian Government Initiative

Now is the best time to invest in a solar battery.

The federal Cheaper Home Batteries Program applies a tiered rebate automatically at the point of installation: $243/kWh on the first 14 kWh, $146/kWh from 15 to 28 kWh, and $36/kWh from 29 to 50 kWh. NSW homeowners can stack the NSW VPP incentive on top, worth up to approximately $1,100. With electricity prices rising and feed-in tariffs falling, the financial case for a home battery has never been stronger.

01

Federal rebate: Cheaper Home Batteries

A tiered rebate is applied automatically to eligible installations nationwide, covering both new systems and batteries added to existing solar. No separate application is required; HelioFlo handles the process end-to-end.

02

NSW VPP incentive on top

NSW homeowners can earn an additional rebate of up to approximately $1,100 by connecting their battery to an approved Virtual Power Plant. All HelioFlo-installed systems are VPP-capable and ready to enrol from day one.

03

Faster return on investment

A well-sized battery saves the average Australian household around $1,500 per year on electricity. With government rebates lowering the entry cost and feed-in tariffs continuing to decline, payback periods are shortening every year.

Battery sizing should match your home's nighttime energy consumption. For most Australian homes, a solar battery in the 10–16 kWh range covers overnight usage comfortably while leaving a reserve for blackout protection. If you're on a time-of-use tariff or want to trade energy back to the grid, a larger system of 16–20 kWh may deliver better returns. We size every solar battery system to your specific usage profile during your free consultation.

LiFePO4 (lithium iron phosphate) is the gold standard chemistry for home solar batteries. Compared to older lithium-ion chemistries, LiFePO4 batteries are significantly safer (no thermal runaway risk), deliver 6,000+ charge cycles, and maintain usable capacity for 15+ years in Australian conditions. All battery systems in the HelioFlo range use LiFePO4 chemistry — chosen specifically for its long-term safety, durability, and performance in residential energy storage.

Yes, when correctly configured for backup operation. All battery systems HelioFlo installs include automatic UPS switching — when the grid goes down, your home transfers to battery power within milliseconds, fast enough that most appliances don't register any interruption. Your solar panels also continue charging the battery during a blackout, so as long as there's sunlight, your energy supply keeps replenishing. We configure backup circuits during installation to protect your essential loads: lights, refrigeration, and other critical devices.
