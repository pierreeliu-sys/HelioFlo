What's Included

Solar self-consumption

Store excess solar energy generated during the day and use it in the evening instead of buying from the grid at peak rates.

LiFePO4 safety

Lithium iron phosphate chemistry is the safest available for residential installation, with no thermal runaway risk and a long, stable cycle life.

Partial blackout backup

Keep essential circuits powered during grid outages. Suitable for lights, refrigeration, and critical appliances.

Government rebate eligible

All HelioFlo-installed batteries are eligible for the federal Cheaper Home Batteries Program rebate, reducing your upfront cost significantly.

Is basic right for you?

Simple storage for smaller needs.

A basic solar battery is ideal when you want to start capturing your solar energy without the cost of a full-scale system. You can always expand later.

Smaller households

1–2 person households or apartments typically consume less than 15 kWh per day, making a 5 kWh battery a meaningful and cost-effective addition.

Lower upfront investment

Basic battery systems carry a lower entry price, making solar storage accessible even when budget is a primary consideration.

Starter energy independence

Stop exporting solar energy for next to nothing. A basic battery captures that energy and uses it when you need it, evening out your grid dependency.

Future expandability

Many basic systems support modular expansion. As your needs or budget grow, you can add capacity without replacing the whole system.
