What's Included

Whole-home overnight coverage

20+ kWh covers the full overnight energy needs of a 5+ person household, even with heating, cooling, and multiple appliances running.

EV charging support

Store surplus solar energy and use it to charge your electric vehicle overnight, effectively giving you free fuel from your own roof.

Full blackout backup (< 4 ms)

Instant whole-home switchover during grid outages. Keep every circuit in your home powered, not just essential loads.

Near-total grid independence

Correctly sized premium systems can achieve up to 95% grid independence, making you virtually immune to electricity price rises.

Modular design

Start at 20 kWh and add modules as your energy demands grow. No need to replace the whole system when you expand.
