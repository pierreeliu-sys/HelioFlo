Key Features

Full overnight coverage

10.24 kWh comfortably covers the overnight energy needs of a 3–4 person household, ensuring you wake up to a full battery ready to be recharged by tomorrow's solar generation.

LiFePO4 chemistry

Lithium iron phosphate is the gold standard for home energy storage: safer than NMC chemistries, zero thermal runaway risk, 6,000+ charge cycles, and 14–16 years of usable service life.

Blackout backup (< 4 ms)

Paired with the Solis Hybrid S6 inverter, the PowerBox Pro switches your home to battery power in under 4 milliseconds during a grid outage, before most appliances even notice.

Modular and expandable

Add additional modules as your energy needs grow, whether from an EV, a pool pump, or simply increasing your self-sufficiency target, without replacing the existing system.

IP65 rated

Designed for indoor and outdoor installation in Australian conditions, including coastal environments. Dust and water resistant for reliable long-term performance.
